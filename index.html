Off-Grid Suitability of Gila County

Uses solar and precipitation data to determine which areas of gila county would be most suitable for off-grid housing. This is done using an estimated 600 sqft of roof space for the solar and water catchment system, with enough water and power to support two people.

Hotspot Analysis of Battles in the Ukraine

Analysis of livemaps gps data from the War in Ukraine, focusing on battles fought between ground forces in 2022. Uses spatial analysis tools to determine hot and cold spot statistics.

Hotpsot Analysis of Explosions in the Ukraine

Analysis of livemaps gps data from the War in Ukraine, focusing on explosions caused by missile and artillery attacks. Uses spatial analysis tools to determine hot and cold spot statistics.
